The BI Group Luxury Amateur Golf

Our Diverse Family

What sets us apart is our commitment to inclusivity. Our team roster reads like a storybook of life experiences, featuring members from various professions, backgrounds, and ages. From seasoned golf veterans to newcomers just discovering the joy of the sport, Team BI Luxury is a tapestry of individuals who share a common passion for golf.

A Symphony of Skills

Within our team, you’ll find a harmonious blend of skill levels, from the seasoned pros who’ve honed their craft over the years to the eager learners who bring fresh perspectives to the game. Together, we create an environment that encourages growth, camaraderie, and the pursuit of excellence.

Our Annual Quest

Each year, we eagerly take to the fairways of the Delhi Golf Club League, where we compete with dedication, heart, and the knowledge that our unity is our greatest strength. It’s not just about the scorecard; it’s about the journey, the friendships forged, and the joy of sharing our love for golf with the world.

Beyond the Greens

Team BI Luxury isn’t just about golf; it’s a community bound by a shared love for the sport and a commitment to camaraderie. We host events, outings, and activities that extend the bonds formed on the course into lasting friendships off it

Join Our Journey

Whether you’re a seasoned golfer looking for a new challenge or a novice eager to take your first swing, Team BI Luxury welcomes you to be a part of our extraordinary journey. Together, we celebrate the sport of golf as a unifying force that transcends backgrounds and brings us closer, one swing at a time.

Join us on the greens, share in our victories, and become a part of the Team BI Luxury family. Together, we elevate the game and the spirit of camaraderie that defines our unique amateur golf team.
