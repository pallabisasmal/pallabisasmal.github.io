Residential

The BI Group comes together with the leader in luxury hospitality – The Oberoi Group to create branded and serviced residential real estate that is a notch above the rest. Enviable locations, iconic contemporary architecture and exclusive amenities marry

the comfort of home with the hassle-free air of a sojourn at a luxury hotel. Our premium properties include Trident Residences, New Delhi, Oberoi Residences at Nandi Hills, Bengaluru and an upcoming project in Nerul, Goa.

Trident Residences, New Delhi

Trident Residences is a first of its kind, conceptualised and developed by BI Luxury Residences in association with The Oberoi Group. Nestled in a bustling neighbourhood in central Delhi, Trident Residences offer an unparalleled urban living

experience. With 19 residences and a host of luxurious amenities set within a verdant landscape, this community is envisaged as an oasis of calm – a quiet retreat that its discerning residents can call home. RERA No. DLRERA2020P0005
